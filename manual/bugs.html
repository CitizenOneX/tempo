Bugs

Topographic maps of EEG recordings could be used as an important tool for diseases diagnostics and treatment. Because of this, it is very important that TEMPO users understand TEMPO license, especially no warranty clause. TEMPO is not thoroughly tested yet and its usage in critical settings is not recommended.

If you encounter something that you think is a TEMPO bug, please report it immediately. Bug reports should be directed to TEMPO mailing list at http://groups.google.com/group/bug-tempo/. Please try to make clear statement of the problem. Suggestions on improving TEMPO are also welcomed.
